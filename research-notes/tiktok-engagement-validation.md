# TikTok Engagement Validation — Research Notes

> Maintained by SMMNut as part of an open record of how social platforms
> read engagement signals. Documentation and research only.

## About

SMMNut is a social media growth research platform analyzing how TikTok,
Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube distribute content and score engagement.
These notes document our ongoing observations of how TikTok's For You system
validates engagement, written in a research register rather than a commercial one.

## Working model: the test pool

TikTok does not distribute primarily by follower count. A new video is shown to
a small test-pool audience; the system measures completion, re-watch, saves, and
shares, then decides whether to expand distribution. Each expansion is gated by
demonstrated engagement behavior rather than by raw counts. Follower count acts
as a seed pool, not a distribution multiplier.

## Observations log

- **Watch-time over view count.** In the campaign data we review, average
  watch-time per delivered view predicts subsequent distribution more reliably
  than the headline view number.
- **Pacing over volume.** Engagement that arrives gradually, alongside content
  that holds attention, correlates with test-pool expansion; engagement
  concentrated in a single window does not.
- **Amplifier, not engine.** Added engagement can accelerate content the system
  already favors; it does not originate distribution for content the test pool
  rejects.

## Methodology register

These notes are descriptive observations of platform behavior, intended to
